Magic Effect

The parallax effect is applied here

The parallax effect is a stunning visual technique that brings depth and dynamism to web design. As you scroll through a webpage, background images move more slowly than the foreground elements, creating an illusion of depth that captivates the viewer. This subtle movement transforms a flat, static page into an engaging and immersive experience, drawing the user’s attention and enhancing the overall aesthetic. By leveraging the parallax effect, we add a layer of sophistication to our designs, making content not only visually appealing but also memorable.
